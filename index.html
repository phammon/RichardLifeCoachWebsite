About Reel Talk Coaching

Reel Talk Coaching was created for men who want more out of life. Men who want more clarity, more strength, more purpose. This space is about real conversations that help men face life's challenges with confidence and resilience.

Sometimes men just need someone to talk to, guy to guy. Reel Talk Coaching provides that safe, non-judgmental space where you can be open without losing your strength. Whether you're going through life transitions, carrying heavy responsibilities, or simply seeking direction, this is your place to unload and recharge.

We start every relationship with a free 15-minute consultation - a judgment-free, commitment-free conversation to see if we're a good fit to work together.

The First Step: A 15-Minute Consultation

Before beginning any coaching sessions, we’ll start with a 15-minute consultation. This is a chance for us to connect and see if we’re a good fit to work together.

During this call, you’ll have the opportunity to:

  • Ask me any questions about coaching, my approach, or the process.
  • Share a little about what brought you here and what you’re looking for.
  • Learn about what sessions look like and how coaching can support you.

This time is completely judgment-free and commitment-free. It’s simply a space for honest conversation to help you decide if Reel Talk Coaching feels right for you. If we both agree it’s a good fit, we’ll set up your first session and begin working together on building clarity, strength, and direction for your life.
